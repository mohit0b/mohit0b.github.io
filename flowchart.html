LinkNode Layered Flow

From Network Mapping to Business Continuity

Visualize how LinkNode ingests supplier data, monitors global risk, propagates impact, and pivots sourcing to keep production lines running without surprises.

1

Data Ingestion & Network Mapping

Top Layer

  • Anchor Registration: The main client creates the primary account and dashboard.
  • Hierarchical Onboarding: Anchor invites Tier 1 → Tier 1 invites Tier 2 → Tier 2 invites Tier 3 raw material sources.
  • Digital Contract Vault: Suppliers upload ISO certifications, ESG compliance records, and SLAs.
  • Global Pool Entry: Independent suppliers join via the open discovery pool.
  • Storage: MongoDB parent-child schema records every node and document.
2

Intelligence & Monitoring

Processing Layer

  • Live Data Fetching: Python risk engine gathers disruption keywords from news and weather APIs while tracking live logistics streams.
  • Geospatial Matching: Disruption radius is compared against supplier GPS coordinates stored in MongoDB.
  • Condition Check: Nodes outside risk zones stay green; impacted suppliers advance to impact analysis.
3

Risk Propagation & Impact Calculation

Analysis Layer

  • Recursive Graph Traversal: Node.js leverages MongoDB $graphLookup to trace the butterfly effect through the chain.
  • Butterfly Logic: Track the path from the affected Tier-3 node up to the anchor and inspect stock buffers at each level.
  • Predictive Metrics: Compute delay days (e.g., 18-day cobalt gap) and production shortfalls (e.g., 500 EVs delayed next month).
4

Mitigation & Resolution

Bottom Layer

  • Dashboard Alerting: UI surfaces red alerts with the impacted contract and severity.
  • Contractual Enforcement: Digital contracts are inspected for penalty clauses and force majeure logic.
  • Alternative Sourcing: Algorithm filters the discovery pool for compliance, lead time, and material grade matches.
  • Pivot Execution: Client approves the backup supplier and establishes a new digital linkage instantly.
Outcome

Business Continuity Maintained

LinkNode converts early warnings into decisive actions, keeping production schedules intact and contracts honored.
